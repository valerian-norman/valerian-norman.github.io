What is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional Intelligence is the ability to understand your own and others emotions and cooperate with others
successfully and have a better understanding of what people are trying to communicate.

What is the difference between IQ and EQ?

EQ is your social ability to understand others and process emotions, while IQ is your problem-solving ability
including memorization and critical thinking.

Emotion Intelligence

Having good Emotional Intelligence (EQ) is important for resolving conflicts and avoid misunderstandings,
not only can it help build relationships with people but it can also improve productivity and help you with your
studies or career. A strong EQ can help you be the best version of yourself, put more thought into your actions
and help you decide what really matters for you.

Colaborative Exercise

Looping is an exercise that you can do with a partner where one person talks for two minutes about a topic and
the second person has to listen. When the first person finishes talking then the second person repeats back what
they understood from the first person. If the second person got something wrong then the first person will clarify
what they got wrong. Repeat this process until both parties have an equal understanding of each other.
