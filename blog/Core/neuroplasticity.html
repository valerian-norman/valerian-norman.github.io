What is neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity is what occurs when you are pacticing an activity and actively trying to improve. You can
learn a topic by studying it, but without practicing it and physically putting your knowledge to use, it will be
hard to carry certain techniques over from short to long term memory. A common misconception about the
brain is that we are born with a certain type of brain that will grow in a set way. But actaully, depending on
what you physically occupy your mind with but also what you think about can change the mental and physical
development of your brain.
